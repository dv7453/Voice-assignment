# Maneuver — Internal Knowledge Base

## Who we are
Maneuver is a performance marketing agency founded in 2021. We work with 
early-to-mid stage startups and D2C brands that have product-market fit 
and are ready to scale. We are a team of 11 — strategists, media buyers, 
designers, and analysts. We are remote-first, based across India and the US.

## What makes us different
Most agencies optimize for impressions. We optimize for revenue. Every 
engagement starts with understanding unit economics — CAC, LTV, payback 
period — before we touch a single ad. We do not take clients we cannot 
grow.

## Services

### 1. Paid Media (Meta + Google)
Full-funnel campaign management across Meta and Google. We handle strategy,
creative briefing, media buying, and weekly reporting. Minimum spend we 
manage: $10,000/month. Our fee: 12% of ad spend + a fixed retainer of 
$2,000/month.

### 2. Growth Strategy Sprint
A 3-week intensive engagement. We audit your current funnel, identify the 
top three growth levers, and deliver a 90-day execution roadmap. Flat fee: 
$8,000. No ongoing commitment required.

### 3. Creative Studio
Performance creative — static ads, video scripts, UGC briefs, landing pages.
Briefed by our strategists, executed by our in-house designers. Delivered 
as a monthly retainer: $3,500/month for up to 20 creative assets.

### 4. Full-Stack Growth Retainer
Our flagship: paid media + creative + strategy under one roof. Reserved for 
brands spending $30,000+/month on ads. Pricing: custom, typically 
$8,000–$15,000/month depending on scope.

## Our process
1. Discovery call — understand the business, goals, current spend, unit economics
2. Audit — we review ad accounts, creatives, landing pages, attribution setup
3. Strategy doc — custom 90-day plan with channel mix, budget allocation, KPIs
4. Onboarding — account access, creative intake, reporting dashboard setup
5. Execution — weekly optimizations, bi-weekly strategy calls, monthly reviews
6. Reporting — custom dashboard, every client gets a dedicated Slack channel

## Case studies

### Nourish (D2C nutrition brand, India)
- Problem: CAC of ₹1,800 on Meta, LTV of ₹2,200. Not profitable at scale.
- What we did: Rebuilt creative strategy around UGC + founder-led content, 
  restructured campaigns from interest to broad + retargeting stack.
- Result: CAC down to ₹980 in 11 weeks. Scaled from ₹4L/month to ₹18L/month 
  ad spend.

### Framestore (SaaS, US)
- Problem: Google Ads burning $40K/month with 4% trial conversion.
- What we did: Rebuilt keyword architecture, rewrote ad copy, redesigned 
  landing pages for ICP segments.
- Result: Trial conversion to 9.2%, CPL down 38% in 8 weeks.

### ThreadCo (fashion D2C, UAE)
- Problem: Entering a new market (India), zero brand awareness, $0 spend.
- What we did: Full-stack retainer from day one. Meta + Google + influencer 
  briefing + landing page localization.
- Result: ₹2.1 Cr revenue in first 6 months, CAC ₹1,100 on a ₹3,800 AOV.
